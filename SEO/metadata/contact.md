## FAQ

**How do I request a bulk quote for school uniforms?**
Fill out the contact form with your organization name and requirements, or email sales@ridhvickapparels.in directly — the sales desk responds within 24 business hours.

**What information should I include in a bulk quote request?**
Your school or organization name, approximate quantity per size, uniform categories needed (primary, high school, sports, accessories), and any custom embroidery or house-color requirements.

**Can I request physical fabric samples before ordering?**
Yes — physical textile fabric swatches can be requested through the contact form under "Physical Textile Fabric Swatches," or by emailing the sales desk directly.

**Does Ridhvick have an office in Chennai as well as Tirupur?**
Yes — the registered office and factory are in Tirupur; a dedicated sales office serves Chennai and the surrounding region.

**How quickly does Ridhvick respond to inquiries?**
The sales desk typically responds within 24 business hours with pricing and sample availability.
