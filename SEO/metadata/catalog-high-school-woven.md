## Structured Page Content

### School Blazer & Woven Uniform Manufacturer for High School

High school uniforms carry more formal weight than primary daily wear — a
blazer or tailored Oxford shirt is often a school's most visible brand
statement to parents and visitors. Ridhvick's woven range is built to match
that: fully-lined construction, structured collars, and reinforced
stitching designed to hold shape through a full academic year, not just look
good on order day.

#### Tailored Academy Blazers
The core blazer is a 70% wool / 30% polyester fully-lined jacket with gold
crest button closures, a water-repellent outer finish, an internal zipper
pocket, and deep functional pockets — priced from ₹3,599. A premium
house-colored variant (Model STL06) adds contrast pocket linings, padded
shoulders, and double-stitched reinforced lapels for schools running a
house-color program, at ₹3,999.

#### Premium Woven Oxford Shirts
The Oxford shirt line uses 100% superfine woven cotton with easy-iron
technology, underarm breathability panels, and reinforced cuffs — built to
take daily wear-and-wash without the fabric breaking down at the collar and
cuff first, which is where cheaper woven shirts usually fail first.

#### House-Colored Blazer Options
Blazers and the matching pleated woven skirt (65% poly / 35% viscose, with
a side safety zipper and heat-set box pleating) are available in
school-specified house colors — navy with gold trim, black with red trim,
and burgundy with silver trim are the current standard options, with custom
color matching available for bulk orders.

#### Crest Embroidery for Blazers & Shirts
Every blazer and shirt in this range is "crest embroidery ready" — meaning
the garment construction accounts for embroidery placement and thread
density from the design stage, rather than embroidery being added as an
afterthought to a finished garment.

## FAQ

**What fabric is used in Ridhvick's school blazers?**
The standard academy blazer uses a 70% wool / 30% polyester blend; the premium house-colored blazer (Model STL06) uses a higher-density 70% wool / 30% polyester blend with padded shoulders and reinforced lapels.

**Can school blazers be custom-colored to match our house system?**
Yes — navy/gold, black/red, and burgundy/silver are standard, with custom color matching available for bulk institutional orders; contact the sales desk with your house colors.

**Do woven shirts and blazers come with crest embroidery included?**
Embroidery is manufactured in the same facility and can be included in the same production run; every blazer and Oxford shirt is designed to accept crest embroidery at the design stage.

**What sizes are available for blazers and Oxford shirts?**
Blazers are available S–XXL; Oxford shirts are available S–XXL as well, with sizing guidance available through the AI Sizing Assistant on each product.

**Is a fabric sample available before a bulk blazer order?**
Yes — physical fabric and stitching samples can be requested ahead of bulk production; contact sales@ridhvickapparels.in.
