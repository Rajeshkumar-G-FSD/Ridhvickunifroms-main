## Structured Page Content

### School Uniform Accessories — Ties, Socks & Backpacks, Bulk Manufactured

Accessories are usually the last thing a school procurement process gets
around to, and the first thing that looks mismatched when it's sourced from
a different vendor than the uniform itself. Ridhvick manufactures the
accessory layer as part of the same order, in matching school colors and
crest branding.

#### School Backpacks
The Ergonomic Crest Backpack is built from 900D ballistic Cordura with
anatomical back-support padding, a laptop sleeve, dual mesh water-bottle
pockets, and a dedicated crest placement panel — designed to survive a full
academic year of daily use, not just look durable on day one.

#### Tie & Socks Sets
The Academy Crest Tie & Socks Set pairs a woven school-logo striping tie
(100% satin silk, pre-tied or self-tie options) with three pairs of
cushioned, arch-supported knit socks (80% cotton, 20% spandex) in
colorfast, non-fading dyes — sold as a bulk-order set rather than requiring
separate tie and sock vendors.

#### Custom Crest Branding on Accessories
Every accessory in this range can carry the same crest embroidery or woven
branding used on the school's blazers and shirts, ordered in the same
production run for color and placement consistency across the whole
uniform.

## FAQ

**What accessories does Ridhvick manufacture alongside school uniforms?**
Backpacks and tie-and-socks sets are the current accessory range, both available with custom school crest branding to match the rest of the uniform order.

**What material is the school backpack made from?**
900D ballistic Cordura — a heavy-duty, water-resistant nylon — with anatomical back-support padding and a laptop sleeve.

**Can the tie and socks be branded with our school crest?**
Yes — the tie uses woven school-logo striping and can be manufactured pre-tied or self-tie; socks are available in colorfast, non-fading dyes to match school colors.

**Can accessories be ordered separately from a uniform order?**
Yes — accessories can be requested as a standalone bulk order, though ordering them alongside a uniform order ensures color and branding consistency in a single production run.

**What sizes are available for the tie and socks set?**
The tie is one standard fit; socks are offered in a standard mid-calf fit — contact the sales desk for age-specific sizing if required for younger students.
