## FAQ

**When was Ridhvick Uniforms founded?**
Ridhvick Apparels was established in 2009 in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu.

**Where is Ridhvick Uniforms manufactured?**
At Ridhvick's own registered facility in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu — India's largest knitwear manufacturing hub — with a second sales office in Chennai.

**Does Ridhvick manufacture uniforms in-house or outsource production?**
In-house — design, fabric production, tailoring, and embroidery are all handled at Ridhvick's own facility.

**What does Ridhvick manufacture besides school uniforms?**
Primarily school and sports uniforms; Ridhvick's broader manufacturing history also includes corporate and institutional apparel (see `keyword-strategy.md` §4 for the recommended `/corporate-uniforms` backlog page).
