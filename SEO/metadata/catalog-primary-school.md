## Structured Page Content

### Primary School Uniform Manufacturer — Kindergarten to Grade 5

Primary-age uniforms take more daily abuse than any other category a school
buys — playground hours, paint, lunch, and a growth spurt every term. Every
Ridhvick primary uniform is built around that reality: pique-cotton polos
with a pill-resistant finish, tartan pinafores with crease-recovery
technology, and Teflon-coated trousers that shed stains before they set.

#### Kindergarten Uniforms
The Kindergarten series (bib-style plaid pinafores, sailor-collar dresses,
raglan polo-and-short packs, knit vests, and pleated scooter skirts) is
designed around elasticated waists and easy independent dressing — a
practical detail that matters more at age 4–5 than at any older grade.

#### Primary Daily Wear
From Grade 1 up, the range shifts to structured daily wear: a signature
pique-cotton contrast-collar polo, a Peter Pan-collar blouse, a
boxer-pleat jumper with adjustable buckles for growth room, and a
flat-front academy trouser with a reinforced double-knee patch. A tartan
kilt, ribbed-cuff sweatshirt, and Oxford long-sleeve shirt with a chest
crest pocket round out the formal and cold-weather options.

#### Fabric & Fit Built for Active Kids
Two fabric families cover the whole primary range: a 220GSM pique
combed-cotton blend for daily polos and blouses, and a poly-viscose
heavy-duty twill for jumpers, kilts, and trousers — chosen for
crease-recovery and colorfastness through frequent washing, not just
initial appearance.

#### Sizing Guide for Growing Children
Every product lists its available size run (typically XS–XL) directly on
the product card; Ridhvick's AI Sizing Assistant (available on every
product's Quick View) recommends a size from a child's height, weight, and
age, with a "growth room" alternate size suggestion — useful for a
mid-year bulk reorder where a parent isn't present to try garments on.

## FAQ

**What's included in Ridhvick's primary school uniform range?**
Polo shirts, plaid tunics and pinafores, boxer-pleat jumpers, tailored trousers, Oxford shirts, cardigans, and a dedicated kindergarten sub-range of pinafores, sailor dresses, and knit vests — sized XS through XL.

**What fabric is used for primary school polos and tunics?**
Daily-wear polos and blouses use a 220GSM pique combed-cotton blend; tunics, jumpers, and trousers use a poly-viscose heavy-duty twill chosen for crease recovery and stain resistance.

**Can primary uniforms be ordered with room for a child to grow?**
Yes — several styles (the boxer-pleat jumper, adjustable-buckle trousers) are built with adjustable growth allowance, and the AI Sizing Assistant suggests an alternate "growth room" size on request.

**Do you manufacture kindergarten uniforms separately from primary?**
Yes, Kindergarten is a distinct product series (pinafores, sailor dresses, active packs, and knit vests) designed around elasticated waists and independent dressing for younger children.

**Can I get a fabric swatch before placing a bulk primary uniform order?**
Yes — physical fabric samples are available on request through the contact form or by emailing sales@ridhvickapparels.in.
