## Structured Page Content

### Interactive Digital Uniform Catalog

Most uniform manufacturers hand over a PDF or a printed spread and expect a
buyer to cross-reference model numbers by email. Ridhvick's digital catalog
keeps the same page-spread format a procurement officer already expects, but
makes it interactive: zoom to inspect weave density and stitch quality,
navigate by topic instead of flipping pages, and select a specific model
straight into a quote — without leaving the page or re-explaining what
"Model PRIM_MDL074" refers to over email.

#### How the Digital Catalog Works
1. **Choose a topic** — Kindergarten, Primary Daily Wear, Sports & House
   Edition, or Woven Academy Series.
2. **Page through the spread** — each page shows multiple models with a
   zoom control (100%–250%) to inspect fabric and construction detail.
3. **Select a model** — see its fabric composition, available colors, and
   size range instantly.
4. **Add to your quote** — choose a size and quantity and add the exact
   model directly to your inquiry package.

This mirrors how a buyer already evaluates a printed catalog, so there's no
new workflow to learn — it just removes the email back-and-forth that
normally follows.

## FAQ

**What is the Ridhvick Digital Catalog?**
An interactive, zoomable version of Ridhvick's physical uniform catalog, organized by topic (Kindergarten, Primary, Sports, Woven), with fabric and sizing detail available on every model.

**Can I add a specific model to a quote request from the digital catalog?**
Yes — selecting a model shows its size and color options; choosing a size and quantity adds that exact model to your bulk quote inquiry.

**Does the digital catalog replace the physical fabric sample?**
No — it's a visual and specification reference; physical fabric swatches can still be requested separately before a bulk order is finalized.

**How many uniform models are in the digital catalog?**
The catalog currently spans four topic series across seven catalog pages, covering kindergarten through woven academy blazers — new models are added as the range expands.
