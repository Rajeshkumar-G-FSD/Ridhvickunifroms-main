## Structured Page Content

### All Service Under One Roof — Uniform Manufacturing & Embroidery

A school procurement officer evaluating uniform vendors is usually really
evaluating risk: will the fabric batch match across a 500-unit order, will
the embroidery placement be consistent, will the delivery date hold. Ridhvick
answers that by keeping every stage in one facility.

#### Product Design & Prototyping
Before bulk cutting begins, Ridhvick delivers digital design previews and
physically stitched fabric samples to the school board — so sizing, color,
and fabric hand can be approved on a real garment, not a rendering.

#### Premium Knitted & Woven Fabric Production
Fabric is produced in-house: high mechanical-stretch poly-viscose and
combed cotton blends for daily wear, aero-knit and CoolMesh performance
polyester for sportswear, and wool-blend woven fabric for blazers —
selected per product for comfort through a full active school day, not
just for initial fabric hand-feel.

#### Precision Custom Embroidery
High thread-density computerized embroidery is run in the same facility as
garment construction, so a school crest or logo is placed and stitched as
part of the same production run — keeping color and placement consistent
across a full bulk order instead of varying batch to batch from a
third-party embroidery vendor.

## FAQ

**Does Ridhvick manufacture uniforms fully in-house, or use subcontractors?**
Every stage — design, fabric weaving/knitting, cutting, stitching, and embroidery — is done in Ridhvick's own Tirupur facility, not outsourced to third-party subcontractors.

**Can we get a physical fabric sample before committing to a bulk order?**
Yes — physically stitched fabric samples are provided as part of the design and prototyping stage, before bulk cutting begins.

**How is custom logo embroidery digitized and applied?**
School logos are digitized and embroidered using computerized, high thread-density embroidery equipment in the same facility as garment production, keeping color and placement consistent across the full order.

**What fabrics does Ridhvick manufacture in-house?**
Poly-viscose and combed cotton blends for daily wear, aero-knit and CoolMesh performance polyester for sportswear, and wool-blend woven fabric for blazers.
