## Structured Page Content (rewritten, SEO-optimized)

### Premium School & Sports Uniforms, Manufactured In-House Since 2009

Ridhvick Uniforms has manufactured school and sports uniforms from its own
production facility in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu — India's largest knitwear
manufacturing cluster — since 2009. Every uniform on this site, from a
primary school polo shirt to a fully-lined academy blazer, is designed,
woven or knitted, cut, stitched, and embroidered under one roof, without
routing work through third-party subcontractors. That matters to a school
procurement officer for one practical reason: a single production line
means a single point of accountability for quality and a single, predictable
timeline for a bulk order — not five vendors' schedules stacked on top of
each other.

#### Daily Wear & Sports Uniforms for Every School Day
The daily wear range covers kindergarten through high school: pique-cotton
polos, tartan pinafores, tailored trousers, and formal Oxford shirts for the
classroom, alongside aero-knit track jackets, CoolMesh shorts, and
moisture-wicking house tees built for PE periods and sports days. Fabric
choices are stated per product — poly-viscose, combed cotton, or performance
polyester — so a buyer can compare on substance, not just a photo.

#### Browse Our Interactive Digital Uniform Catalog
Rather than a static PDF, Ridhvick's digital catalog lets a school
representative zoom into stitch and weave detail, page through model series
by topic (Kindergarten, Primary Daily Wear, Sports & House Edition, Woven
Academy Series), and add a specific model, size, and quantity straight to a
quote request. No competitor manufacturer reviewed during this SEO audit
offers an equivalent interactive tool.

#### Explore Sizing & Fabrics — Full Uniform Catalog
The full catalog is filterable by Primary School, High School, Sports Wear,
and Accessories, with a fabric composition, feature list, and size range
listed on every item — the same level of detail a buyer would otherwise have
to request by email.

#### All Service Under One Roof — Our Manufacturing Process
1. **Product Design & Prototyping** — digital design previews and physically
   stitched fabric samples go to the school board before any bulk cutting
   begins.
2. **Premium Knitted & Woven Fabrics** — high-stretch poly-viscose and
   combed cotton, chosen for comfort through a full active school day.
3. **Precision Custom Embroidery** — high thread-density computerized
   embroidery keeps school crests crisp and colorfast through repeated
   washing, run in the same facility as the garment itself.

#### Let's Connect — Request Your Bulk Quote
A school or organization can build a package directly from the catalog,
specify custom embroidery needs, and submit an inquiry that reaches the
Ridhvick sales desk within 24 business hours — or contact the Tirupur or
Chennai office directly (see Contact page).

## FAQ (verbatim match to `schema/faq.json` — do not let these drift apart)

**Who manufactures Ridhvick school uniforms and where are they made?**
Ridhvick Uniforms is a school and sports uniform manufacturer based in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu — India's largest knitwear manufacturing hub. Ridhvick has designed, woven, and embroidered uniforms in-house at its own facility since 2009, with a second sales office in Chennai.

**What types of school uniforms does Ridhvick manufacture?**
Ridhvick manufactures the full range a school needs from a single vendor: kindergarten and primary daily wear (polos, tunics, pinafores, jumpers), high school woven uniforms and tailored blazers, sports and house-color athletic wear, and accessories such as ties, socks, and backpacks.

**Does Ridhvick offer custom logo embroidery for school uniforms?**
Yes. Ridhvick runs computerized, high thread-density embroidery in-house so school crests and logos stay crisp, colorfast, and durable through repeated washing. Digitizing setup and chest-logo placement are handled as part of the same production run as the uniform itself, with no third-party embroidery vendor involved.

**Can Ridhvick handle bulk school uniform orders for an entire institution?**
Yes — bulk institutional and school-wide orders are Ridhvick's core business. Every stage, from thread-blend trials and physical fabric sampling to cutting, stitching, embroidery, and regional distribution, is managed under one roof, which keeps bulk orders on a single predictable production timeline instead of being split across subcontractors.

**How do I get a price quote or request physical fabric samples?**
Add the uniforms you need to your inquiry package on the Ridhvick website and submit the request form with your school or organization name, or email sales@ridhvickapparels.in directly. The Ridhvick sales desk responds with pricing and physical fabric swatch availability within 24 business hours.

**What fabrics are used in Ridhvick's sports uniforms?**
Ridhvick's sports and athletic wear line uses aero-knit moisture-wicking performance polyester, breathable CoolMesh panels, and heavyweight interlock knit for track jackets — chosen specifically for ventilation and quick-dry performance during school sports periods.

## EEAT Notes
- **Experience:** "since 2009," specific fabric names, and real product
  specs (already present in `uniforms.ts`) are genuine experience signals —
  keep them prominent, don't generalize them away in future copy edits.
- **Expertise:** the Manufacturing page's step-by-step process description
  is the strongest expertise signal on the site — link to it from every
  other page, not just the nav.
- **Authoritativeness:** currently weak — no third-party citations, press
  mentions, or verified certifications are referenced anywhere. Lowest-cost
  fix: get listed/verified on Google Business Profile and IndiaMART (already
  has a listing — see README) and link to both from the site.
- **Trust:** real registered address, two real phone numbers, and two real
  email addresses are present — good. Missing: a stated MOQ, lead time, and
  return/sample policy, which is what a first-time institutional buyer
  actually checks before trusting a manufacturer with a bulk order.
