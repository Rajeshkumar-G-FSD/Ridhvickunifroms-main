## Structured Page Content

### Sports Uniform Manufacturer for Schools — Athletic Wear & House Kits

A sports uniform fails differently than a daily uniform: it fails on the
field, mid-match, in front of parents. Ridhvick's sports range is built
specifically around moisture management and range of motion, not adapted
from daily-wear fabric.

#### Track Jackets & Athletic Outerwear
The knitted athletic track jacket uses aero-knit performance polyester with
a full front zip, thermal insulation layer, and reflective safety stripes.
The house-series variant adds a heavyweight interlock knit shell, twin
zippered pockets, and a stand-up mock collar — built for outdoor practice in
cooler weather without sacrificing breathability during activity.

#### Moisture-Wicking Tees & Shorts
The active panel raglan tee uses Aero-Dry performance polyester mesh with
side-ventilating panels and contrast raglan sleeves for shoulder mobility.
Matching shorts use a 100% recycled CoolMesh fabric with an elastic
drawstring waist, anti-odor treatment, and interlock weave support —
engineered to stay dry and odor-controlled through a full PE period, not
just look athletic.

#### House-Color Sports Kits
Raglan tees and track jackets are available in coordinated house colors
(Ruby Red, Emerald Green, Topaz Yellow, Sapphire Blue for tees; Navy/Yellow,
Navy/Red, Green/White for jackets) so a school's sports-day house system can
be manufactured as a matched set rather than assembled from separate orders.

## FAQ

**What fabric do Ridhvick sports uniforms use?**
Aero-knit performance polyester for track jackets, 100% recycled CoolMesh for shorts, and Aero-Dry performance polyester mesh for raglan tees — all chosen for moisture-wicking and quick-dry performance.

**Can sports uniforms be manufactured in our school's house colors?**
Yes — raglan tees and track jackets are available in multiple house-color combinations, with custom color matching available for bulk institutional orders.

**Are Ridhvick sports uniforms suitable for outdoor athletics programs?**
Yes — the track jacket line includes a thermal insulation layer and reflective safety stripes for outdoor practice and low-light visibility, in addition to the moisture-wicking daily-PE range.

**What sizes are available for sports uniforms?**
Track jackets and tees are available XS–XL (house-series jackets to XXL); shorts are available XS–XL.

**Can we order a matched sports kit (jacket, tee, and shorts) as one set?**
Yes — house-series kits can be ordered as a coordinated set in matching colorways through a single bulk quote request.
